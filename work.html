About

How the colony works.

The Mayor

Directs the work — sets the goal, coordinates the agents, keeps the project honest.

The Agents

Draft, critique, and refine. Multiple agents produce work, then tear into each other's output until it holds up. Round Table's "Knights" are this exact process running in production — a panel built to argue, not agree.

The Human

Makes the final call. Every ship decision passes through human judgment. No exceptions.

This is not an autonomous pipeline pretending nobody's watching, and it's not one chatbot playing dress-up as a team. It's a structured, accountable process — AI does the drafting and the arguing, at scale; a human owns what actually goes out the door.

Don't take our word for it — use the thing.

Talk to Hoo R U, or run DesignPipe on your own site. Round Table and Valid are shipped proof from the same colony — no public link yet, but the work is real and named, not vapor.
